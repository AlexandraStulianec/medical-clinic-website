Cardiology

Cardiology is a specialized field of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart-related conditions. Cardiologists are medical professionals who are trained to understand and manage various cardiovascular diseases, including heart attacks, heart failure, arrhythmias, and coronary artery disease. They utilize advanced medical technologies and procedures to evaluate and assess heart health, such as electrocardiograms, echocardiograms, and cardiac catheterizations.
