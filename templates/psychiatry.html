Psychiatry

Psychiatry is a medical specialty that deals with the diagnosis, treatment, and management of mental disorders. Psychiatrists are medical doctors who specialize in understanding the complexities of the human mind and addressing mental health concerns. They evaluate patients through interviews, psychological assessments, and observation to diagnose conditions such as depression, anxiety disorders, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and substance abuse disorders. Psychiatrists use various treatment modalities, including medications, psychotherapy, and other therapeutic interventions, to help patients manage their mental health conditions.
