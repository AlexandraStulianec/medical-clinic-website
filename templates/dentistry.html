Dentistry

Dentistry encompasses the evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment of oral health issues. Dentists are oral healthcare professionals who focus on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting the teeth, gums, and mouth. They perform routine dental examinations, cleanings, and X-rays to identify dental problems like tooth decay, gum disease, or oral infections. Dentists provide various treatments, including fillings, extractions, root canals, and dental prosthetics like dentures or implants.
